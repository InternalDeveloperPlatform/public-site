# LocalOps

LocalOps is an AI-powered Internal Developer Platform that gives your engineers a Heroku-like self-serve experience on your own cloud — so they ship 3x faster, incidents resolve in minutes, and you never need to hire a DevOps team.

Engineers get:

- Production-grade infrastructure on any cloud in minutes — not weeks
- Deploy any stack, any architecture, without DevOps dependency
- Full observability built-in — eliminate DataDog and NewRelic spend
- Security and encryption pre-configured — audit-ready from day one
- Auto-scaling on demand — no 2am PagerDuty calls
- PR preview environments — catch bugs before they reach production
- AI SRE agents — mean time to resolution drops from hours to minutes
